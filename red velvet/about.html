About Moonpie Company We lovingly create our soaps using the finest quality organic vegetable oils and butters. Our soaps are handcrafted in small batches and individually hand-cut so that no two bars are exactly alike. We only sell soap that meets our high standards and has been personally tested by us. We source many of our ingredients locally and strive to help support other local small businesses. Free shipping on orders of $75 or more! Just enter FREE SHIPPING on the checkout page.
